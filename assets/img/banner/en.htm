Why Fonon?

Fonon brand products are produced at "Gold Moon Tashkent", the largest jewelry factory in Central Asia. The company cooperates with leading countries in the field of jewelry, such as Italy, Germany and Turkey. The high-capacity factory offers a wide selection for all jewelry enthusiasts. Rings, bracelets, gold chains, necklaces, pendants, jewelry under the Fonon brand - each product is created at the level of a work of art.
